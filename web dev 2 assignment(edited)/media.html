Media Collection

Welcome to the media section of my portfolio. This page contains links and an overview of media projects undertaken by myself as part of Creative Digital Media. These videos were created as part of the module Introduction to Digital Media. The primary software used for both of these videos was IMovie.

I feel that through creating these videos I have greatly developed my video editing skills and improved my understanding of copyright laws.

I hope to create more videos in the near future and build upon these skills.

Nelson Mandela-The Man Who Freed A Nation

Click here to view this video

This video was created as a historical piece exploring the life of Nelson Mandela, from his release to his presidency. As a worldwide icon and personal hero of mine, my aim was to show how Mandela ultimately changed South Africa for the better, through ending apartheid and uniting a nation. This short video was created using the software, Imovie along with resources such as Google Images and Youtube for resources. To view this video click on the link above or the image below.

Castleknock Flowers Advertisement Video

Advertisement Video for Castleknock Flowers

This video was created as a means of promoting the local florists, Castleknock Flowers. This video was created using IMovie. The footage for this video was shot using a standard DSLR camera. The idea behind this video was to explore a number of different video production tecniques, such as panning etc. The music used in this video is courtesy of Copyright Free Sounds.
